Weiss teaching philosophy

 

Lessons are tailored to your needs. I am available to work with beginning flutists, as well as intermediate and advanced players. We can work on basic repertoire, band/orchestra/ensemble music, preparation for recitals or solo and ensemble competitions, preparation for college music auditions.

I am also available to assist semi professional and professional musicians in preparation for major performances and/or auditions, and to work with musicians who wish to overcome performance anxiety issues. I am currently available for lessons in West Los Angeles, and occasional coaching in Portland, Oregon.
